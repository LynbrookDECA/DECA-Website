About Us

Who Are We?

Not only do we focus on competing at conferences, but we also provide members with the opportunity to discover business and leadership through community service events, socials, company tours, and speaker events.

CTE Requirement

It is important to note that this business course must be on the student's high school transcript, and you only need to take a business course once (if you already took one, then no need to do anything; if not, you must enroll in a CTE course to compete at conferences).
